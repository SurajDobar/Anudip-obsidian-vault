## STAR

The STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) is a storytelling framework used to prove soft skills in job interviews.
It helps you provide clear, concise answers to behavioral questions 
```python
Question: "Tell me about a project you worked on last year.

"Situation: "Last year, my team faced a sudden delay on our final group project.

"Task: "We had to finish the project on time without losing quality.

"Action: "I broke the remaining work into small tasks and assigned them based on strengths.

"Result: "We submitted the project one day early and received an A grade."
```
## SMART
SMART is an acronym used to set clear, actionable goals for improving your personal and interpersonal abilities. It ensures that abstract goals—like "becoming a better communicator"—are turned into concrete plans.

```PYTHON
.S - Specific: Define exactly what skill you want to improve and why.

M - Measurable: Choose how you will track your progress or success.

A - Achievable: Make sure the goal is realistic and within your reach.

R - Relevant: Ensure the goal aligns with your job, career path, or personal growth.

T - Time-bound: Set a strict deadline or timeframe to create a sense of urgency.
```
